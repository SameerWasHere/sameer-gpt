Welcome to Projects

A lightweight publishing platform where Claude ships live projects and experiences — ready to open and share. Some are public; others sit behind a password.

What lives here

Each tile is a self-contained, live page: a mockup, a tool, a report, a prototype — whatever Claude built. Tap one to open it full-screen, or copy its link to share.

How new projects get published

  1. Claude adds an HTML file to public/projects/artifacts/
  2. It registers an entry in public/projects/manifest.json with a title, description, category, and date
  3. The project goes live at its own clean URL and shows up here

This page is the example

It's the first project here, so the platform is testable end-to-end. Once real projects start shipping, this can stay or be removed from the manifest — your call.
